Standard Edition:

  This is an our Software as a service version of the platform for those corporates or institutions that would require online assessments their your recruitment, training, examination needs without any installation or maintainence. You'll need little or almost no training to start using our platform because it is a self service SaaS that is easy to understand and operate.

Professional Edition :

  The professional edition provide all the features of standard edition and also allow corporates or institutions to customize their assement portal with their own brand and also provide additional features such as outcome based hiring, skills mastery analysis, CSV report data download for fruther analysis, batch data import/export, API Integration, Integration with with the market leading applicant tracking systems to providing a seamless experience for candidates and technical continuity for your selection process.

Enterprise Edition :

  The Enterprise edition provide all the features of professional edition and can be hosted with clients own domain names. We would also provide further customization as per client request to meet the customers online assessments. The assessments can also take using our own secure desktop client application that is designed to help organizations provide secure environment in which to deliver high stakes assessments such as tests and exams.
